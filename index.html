About Me

I am a passionate developer and data enthusiast currently pursuing a dual path: a Bachelor of Software Engineering at the Open University of Sri Lanka and a B.Sc. in Information Technology at the Rajarata University of Sri Lanka. I thrive at the intersection of robust code, intelligent data, and community impact.
